EMO-vest facilitates emergency assistance for epilepsy patients while also providing protection to reduce the risk of injury during seizures. Its development also promotes the use of the Internet of Things (IoT) to advance science and technology in the healthcare field.

## 📌 Summary

Epilepsy is a neurological condition characterized primarily by seizures and sudden loss of consciousness, which can be life-threatening if not treated promptly. To address this issue, our team has developed EMO-vest, an Internet of Things (IoT)-based monitoring and protective device that detects changes in tilt acceleration and vibrations during epileptic seizures. The device is equipped with features such as SMS alerts with the patient’s location, phone call notifications, a buzzer, LED indicators, and an automatic airbag to protect the patient’s vital areas, enabling individuals with epilepsy to carry out daily activities more safely and independently.
